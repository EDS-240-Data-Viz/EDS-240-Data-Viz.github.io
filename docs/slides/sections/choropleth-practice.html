METHOD 1: EQUAL INTERVALS - boundaries will enclose equal ranges of data (e.g. 0-10, 11-20, 21-30, etc.) - works well when frequencies are constant - to calculate breaks: get max and min precipitations, subtract them, divide result by number of classes (intervals) that you wat
